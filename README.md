## 🌷 About Me

Hi! I'm **Stella** — a Frontend Design & Development graduate and intern with a love for creating beautiful, accessible, and user-centred digital experiences.

I specialise in **UX/UI Design** and **Web Accessibility**, and I care deeply about building interfaces that are not only visually delightful but inclusive and usable for everyone. Whether I'm crafting pixel-perfect designs in Figma or writing semantic, accessible HTML, I bring thoughtfulness to every step of the process.

If you'd like to connect or collaborate, don't hesitate to send me a message — I'd love to hear from you! 💌
